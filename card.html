FEATURES & APPLICATIONS

Firestop acrylic sealant / RT-606

FEATURES

  • Adheres to concrete, drywall and masonry
  • Approval for virtually any transit type – cables, trays, conduits and pipes (metal and plastic)
  • Suitable for complex and difficult-to-reach applications – single-sided installation possible and no reinforcing material needed
  • Fast curing in less than 10 minutes
  • Mold, mildew, water and vapor resistant

APPLICATIONS

  • Adheres to concrete, drywall and masonry
  • Approval for virtually any transit type – cables, trays, conduits and pipes (metal and plastic)
  • Suitable for complex and difficult-to-reach applications – single-sided installation possible and no reinforcing material needed
  • Fast curing in less than 10 minutes
  • Mold, mildew, water and vapor resistant
